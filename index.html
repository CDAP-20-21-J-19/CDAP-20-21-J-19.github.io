Emotion Tracking

Captures your face via the Camera

Making use of modern technologies, The Facial extraction system helps to adjust the difficulty of the mobile game according to the user's emotions.
The emotion extraction part can extract the user's emotion using the camera and a neural network while the user plays the game, without interrupting the game.

Lifestyle Tracking

Uses smartwatch to identify your activities

By monitoring the accelerometer of the smartWatch, the app can use this data in order to predict your activities. By processing these activities the app can identify any unhealthy health practices.
Once an unhealthy practice is noticed, the app will send you a notification. So you can get rid of these unhealthy practices and maintain your long-term health.

Diet Tracking

Just take a picture of your Food

Identifies the calories of the food and will generate a diet plan for you. The diet plan will dynamically adjust according to your behaviour and food intake.
Will work in conjunction with your BMI readings, assisting you in maintaining your health. In case you deviate from the given diet plan, the app will take those calories into consideration and update your plan.
